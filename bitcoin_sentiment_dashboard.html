hidden patterns discovered
Contrarian signal
Fear is an underrated entry
Avg PnL during Fear ($54.29) nearly matches Extreme Greed. Savvy traders likely enter oversold positions and capture strong reversals. Consider increasing position size when the index enters the 15–35 range.
Momentum signal
Extreme Greed = peak alpha zone
Highest avg PnL at $67.89 during Extreme Greed. Momentum strategies thrive in euphoric markets. Ride the trend — but set tighter trailing stops as the index approaches 80+ to lock in gains.
Avoidance signal
Neutral zones are value traps
Lowest avg PnL ($34.31) in Neutral markets. Choppy, directionless price action reduces edge. Scale back size or switch to shorter timeframes during 45–55 index readings to reduce unnecessary drawdown.
actionable strategy framework
01
Sentiment-gated position sizing
Use full position size (100%) during Extreme Greed and Fear phases. Reduce to 50–60% during Neutral and Extreme Fear. This single filter could raise risk-adjusted returns significantly based on the $33+ PnL differential observed between best and worst zones.
02
Contrarian entries in Fear zones
When the index drops below 25 (Fear/Extreme Fear), look for long setups on BTC and major alts. Historical PnL data suggests these periods reward patient buyers — particularly when fear persists for 3+ consecutive days.
03
Momentum harvesting in Greed
During Greed (index 60–80), trend-following strategies outperform. Use breakout entries with trailing stops rather than fixed targets to capture extended sentiment-driven moves that often persist longer than expected.
04
Extreme Fear = risk management mode
Despite a decent average PnL ($34.54), Extreme Fear has high variance with large drawdowns possible. Use tighter stop-losses, avoid overleveraging, and prefer mean-reversion over trend-following during these high-uncertainty periods.
